Fighting COVID-19 In Israel

weHelp is a non for profit charity founded in order to assist medical personnel and local restaurant businesses during the Covid-19 crisis.

Our Mission - to assist both struggling businesses and our brave and hardworking medical staff by utilizing all proceeds to purchase hot meals from restaurants that have seen great economic hardship due to Covid-19, and to deliver these meals to medical personnel to Israeli hospitals.
